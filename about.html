About Cambria


Rev. Alex Cain and Family

Cambria is...

  • Cambria is a Christ Centered Church!
  • Cambria is a conservative, Bible believing congregation of believers who emphasize literal interpretation and eternal security.
  • Cambria is a talented church consisting of many gifted singers, musicians and teachers who all seek to give God the glory for their abilities.
  • Cambria lets the Lord lead in its services and doesn’t amber itself with a worship schedule or itinerary.
  • Cambria takes a strong stand against abortion, alcohol, drugs, premarital sex, homosexuality and violence.
  • Cambria is a united church with a common goal – seeking the salvation of all the lost, regardless of sex, race, or social status.
  • Cambria is a giving church that seeks to provide a helping hand to the needy and less privileged in the surrounding area.
  • Cambria is an accessible church. It is located in a convenient area just off U.S. Highway 411 and County Road 875. Facilities are equipped for use by those with a disability.
