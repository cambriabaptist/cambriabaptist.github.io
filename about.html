About Cambria


Rev. Eddie Rowland

From Our Pastor

I am Eddie Rowland, Pastor of Cambria Baptist Church. The members of this church are Christian people that represent what make our church great. They are talented individuals who seek to glorify God and spread the Gospel of His son Jesus Christ. That is what Cambria is all about, worshiping and glorifying the Lord.

Through our Sunday school and Bible Study, Cambria teaches the Word of God. Through our Worship Services we praise God and spread His Gospel. We also provide opportunities for Christian fellowship with our many cook-outs, picnics, and field days.

Cambria is a Church that believes in the truths of the King James Version of the Holy Bible. We believe that Jesus is the Son of God, that He lived a sinless life, died on the cross to pay for our sins, and rose from the grave to ascend to the right hand of the Father. We hope that we are able to share this belief with those who have not yet experienced salvation by the grace of God.
